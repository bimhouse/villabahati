10 Guests

There are rooms to comfortably fit ten adults.

Entire Home

You will have the whole house and property to yourself.

Fenced Yard

There is a private yard with gated access.

Private Pool

You have a private swimming pool in the backyard. It is regularly maintained by a pool service.

High-Speed Internet

Internet is avaiable, with multiple wifi access points in the house.

Laptop Friendly Workspace

There is a secretary desk in the master suite, with access to wifi, should you need to work.

Air Conditioning

There are air-conditioning units in each bedroom and four units in the main living area, all with remotes. Ceiling fans are also located in each room.

Monitored Utilities

Utilities are metered and billed by consumption. The dryer runs on gas. The hot water runs off solar energy! We take care of all costs within reason. Should electricity usage be abused, we will charge you for overuse.

Free Parking

There are two parking spaces on the driveway. Parking is also available on the street.

Essentials

Towels, bedsheets, soap and toilet paper.

Living Room

Large, comfortable room with a massive sofa, fans and air conditioning. It is the perfect place to watch TV, play a board game or read a book.

Kitchen

The kitchen has a gas stove with 10 burners and two ovens, a double fridge/freezer and comes complete with everything you would need to cook a delicious meal.

Dining Room Table

There is enough table space and chairs to seat 14 guests (two extra extensions can be added to the table.

Coffee

There are two drip coffee makers both small and large, an espresso machine and a French Press.

Small Appliances

The kitchen is equipped with a Ninja blender for making smoothies, a margarita blender, a Kitchen Aid mixer, a waffle maker and a kettle.

2 Televisions

There is a 65" 4K television in the living room and a 55" 4K television in the master suite with Apple TV & local Aruban television channels.

Smart Lock

Phone enabled lock allows for easy access to and from the property. We will provide you the access code.

Monitored Security

Cameras monitor the front door and parking driveway.

Washer & Dryer

A laundry room, complete with washer, dryer and sink.

Hair Dryers

You have a hair dryer in each ensuite bathroom.

5 Bedrooms

There are five bedrooms, each with a kingsize bed with white linens, closet, chest of drawers and ensuite bath.

5.5 Bathrooms

There is an ensuite in each bedroom with showers and dual sinks. The master ensuite has a freestanding tub. Powder room for guests.
