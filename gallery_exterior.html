Gallery - Exterior

Architecture

Claudio drew up the plans for this house himself, carefully designing every aspect. The exterior includes stone planters, lava rock, a handcarved wood door and tones of white rock all imported from Bali. The house is completed with white walls and striking black details.

Garden Oasis

The backyard at Bahati, called the Garden Oasis is quite possibly the most used area of the whole house. Everything looks great thanks to the clean linear design. This area of the property is fenced for privacy. THere is a pool, yoga corner, a seating area and the Bali Bed. The space is also decorated with plants and various treasures from our walks along the shorelines of Aruba.

Natural Beauty

The attention to detail made it into the design of the Garden Oasis as well. Michele-Anne handpicked most of the stone items in the backyard from her trip to Bali. There is a relaxing fountain and luscious plants placed thoughout the space giving it a natural healing beauty.

Room to Spare

There is plenty of room outside for everybody, so go ahead and bring guests over for dinner. You will find a built in gas barbecue, equiped with lights for late night grilling. The place is also fully lit so you can continue entertaining well into the evening.

There is a patio dining table that seats 8 guests. A living room like area is located at the back fence, greatfor entertaining. There is also a private garden off to the sideyard, called the Scotch Garden, it is a great place to read and relax away from the all the noise.

Bahati has a pool, plenty of room to enjoy the sun and even a secluded corner to sit and read, we think that you might even spend the majority of your time here outside.
