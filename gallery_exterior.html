Gallery - Exterior

Creativity

The owners, Claudio the architect, had a clear eye on how he wanted to design the house and Michèle-Anne, the creative input, added all the finishing touches. They teamed up to design every aspect of Bahati. The house modern lines is enhanced by white walls and striking black details. The exterior includes tropical landscaping with stone planters, lava rock, a handcarved wood door and tonnes of white rock all imported from Bali.

It's all in the details...

Garden Oasis

The backyard at Bahati, called the Garden Oasis is quite possibly the most used area of this property. The clean linear design of the exterior, the pool, yoga corner, the Bali Bed, the Bali Bench, all the lounge chairs, bean bags and cosy seating areas are perfect to relax and enjoy at any time of the day or night. What makes it perfect is a white wall that outlines the property, giving it all the privacy and safety that you need.

Natural Beauty

The attention to detail made it into the design of the Garden. Michele-Anne brought back from Bali, fountain, planters, statues, Bali Bed and garden furniture. Built the back wall to include the big rock. Bought lots of plants that the neighbourhood goats enjoyed before the wall was built. Added the extra details by exploring the beaches of Aruba, there we found the driftwood for the posts of the yoga corner, as well as the shells, the rocks and the sea fans. All these details make the different areas of the garden the zen sanctuary it is!

Dine Al Fresco

You can enjoy your meals outside in the covered patio at a table that seats ten. Or enjoy a cosy tête-à-tête in the fountain (Scotch) garden.

There is a Napoleon BBQ for all your grilling needs that is well lit for night time barbecuing. Private chefs can be requested should you not fancy cooking or going out!

The Scotch Garden

The Scotch garden is what Claudio calls his area, it is the perfect place to enjoy a Scotch after dinner.

The zen garden or fountain garden are other names for this idyllic, lush private corner of the property. It is also a great place to enjoy an early morning coffee.

Bahati has plenty of room to enjoy the sun. Refresh in the pool. Relax on the cosy Bali Bed or center yourself in the yoga retreat. There is even room for the kids to run around. You might enjoy Bahati so much that you will not want to leave!
