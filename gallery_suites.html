Langata - The Master Suite

Langata is located across the Nairobi wildlife reserve, a place where leopards chuff and lions roar at night. This is the place where we got married in a beautiful garden and called home for over a quarter century.

This room has a private washroom. With a shower, a bathtub, and a private walk in closet. It also has it's own private sliding door to access the backyard, making it the perfect place to hide away from the rest of the house and relax.

Private Quarters

The entire master suite, closet and bathroom are tucked into a quiet corner of the house, making it the go-to spot for relaxation.

Kilifi

Set on Kenya's north coast, Kilifi is a place that holds so many memories for us. You could sit all night by the pool and listen to the waves crash on the beach. When you would look up, the sky was sprinkled with a thousand tiny stars, it felt like you could reach out and touch them.

Wasini

A short boat ride, a Dhow, on the Indian Ocean with dolphins playing in the wake to reach this pristine island with white sand and crystal clear water.

Diani

One has to take an old ferry to reach the southern coast of Kenya. The cars line up patiently waiting to board, while street vendors with trays balanced on their heads walk between the cars selling everything from newspaper cones filled with peanuts to fresh coconut water. Diani also has miles and miles of pristine white sand beaches that line an aquamarine sea. Just like Aruba, it is a place that no one ever wants to leave.

Watamu

"I could not convince myself that we had come to such a wonderful place, a country from which we had to come out of a dream, happy to have dreamed." - Ernest Hemingway. With the most magical of coral reefs, there is a multitude of colourful fish and marine life that swim freely in a deep blue sea.
