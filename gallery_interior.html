Kitchen & Dining Room

Humans are social animals, we enjoy watching the cook at work while having a nice cocktail or enjoying a glass of wine. To enjoy the experience of cooking, Bahati offers the perfect utensils, dishes for serving and the right appliances. There is a large table, with two extensions available, ghost chairs and a small bench which will comfortably accommodate up to 14 guests. All that is missing are family and friends to lend a helping hand.
